OUR MISSION AND VISION

Our mission is to bring to light the topics of male abuse. The change we are looking for is to alter the societal view of the male figure to something that can be emotional and be affected by tragic events just as greatly as women. Men should not have to feel as though they are meant to feel nothing in the face of violence and abuse.

Our vision is to reveal to the general populous the hardships so many men face everyday that are not thought to be common. We strive to create a more accepting and gentler society by offering solutions and advice on topics that many are not comfortable discussing. This includes, but not limited to, domestic violence, sexual assault, human trafficking, and drug abuse with the focus on men in such sit- uations. Our goals include educating the general public while also creating a safe environment for those affected by such tragedy and to be a voice for those that are voiceless in the face of violence.
